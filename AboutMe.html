Who Am I

I’m someone who loves adventure and feels alive when I’m playing sports, riding motorcycles or checking out cars. Life on a farm surrounded by animals keeps me grounded and connected to nature. I’m humble, but I take pride in being a pure-blooded Igorot with a touch of Chinese heritage. Learning new skills excites me and I believe real growth comes from doing, not just reading. I enjoy experiences more than knowledge on paper because they shape who I am. I’m curious about the world and always looking for new challenges to push myself. I try to stay true to my roots while exploring everything life has to offer. I value simplicity, honesty and moments that make life feel meaningful. I love the balance between thrill and peace fast rides and quiet farm mornings. At the core, I live curious, stay real and strive to make every moment count.
